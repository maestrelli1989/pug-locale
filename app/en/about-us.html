About us

UAPAY is a national payment service that provides financial and technical solutions for any type of online business. Our team of professionals will help you automate payments and speed up business processes.

The company, founded in 2013, offers competitive rates and guarantees reliability. We care about the security and speed of online payment, so we try to use the latest technologies that provide the required level of payments.

A high level of service, transparency and reliability of transactions, prompt and qualified work of a support service - all this allowed us to gain the trust of our customers and secure the image of an honest and stable company.
